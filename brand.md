# Brand — Gildore Arena

_Status: initial_

Gildore Arena uses a dark, professional trading-terminal feel with restrained gold accents. The UI should feel like a serious market research workspace rather than a generic crypto landing page.

## Palette

- Background: near-black graphite.
- Surface: dark charcoal panels.
- Accent: muted gold for primary action, fib lines, and active strategy state.
- Secondary accent: soft silver for supporting labels and neutral market annotations.
- Success: calm green for valid setups and profitable position state.
- Danger: muted red for stop loss, invalidation, and risk warnings.

## Typography

- Use a system sans-serif stack for speed and clarity.
- Large headlines should be compact and direct.
- Numeric trading state should be easy to scan.

## Voice

- Precise.
- Trading-native.
- Avoid hype.
- Be clear that MVP trades are simulated and backend-tracked.
