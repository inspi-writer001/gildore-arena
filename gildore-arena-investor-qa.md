# Gildore Arena — Crypto Angel Q&A Prep

## Why won't users just use a signal group or copy-trading product?

> Signal groups sell a conclusion. Gildore shows the setup, risk, invalidation, and subsequent execution trail so users can judge consistency before funding an agent. Copy-trading can execute a trade, but it does not make the decision process inspectable or constrain an agent-specific funding allowance in the same way.

## Why does this need Solana?

> Gildore needs controlled funding, verifiable execution, and settlement to share one rail. The Solana vault program gives each user an agent-specific state and spend allowance; FlashTrade supplies the perpetuals venue; the arena persists the lifecycle for users to inspect.

## What has been proven so far?

> The product, Solana vault architecture, FlashTrade v2 execution layer, and Celo-to-Solana USDC route are built. We have more than 100 Telegram members after a promotional video, but we do not present this as product traction; the next proof is completed user funding and settlement cycles.

## What is the riskiest assumption?

> The main risk is whether users will repeatedly fund agents because the transparency creates enough trust and value. The $100K round is designed to test that directly with a measurable discovery → funding → execution → settlement loop, not to fund broad expansion before the behavior is proven.

## How do you make money?

> The vault architecture supports a capped platform-fee configuration, and the product can later test transparent participation fees and premium agent access. We are deliberately not claiming revenue or unit economics before the first activation loop is measured.

## Why FlashTrade instead of building a perp venue?

> Building a venue would distract from the wedge: agent transparency and capital controls. FlashTrade is a Solana-native perp venue with the required USDC collateral and position lifecycle primitives; Gildore builds the user-facing inspection and controlled-execution layer on top.

## What is the token's role in this round?

> None. The token was fairly launched independently, and the founder's purchased tokens are locked. This is an equity-only post-money SAFE with no token rights, warrants, allocation, TGE unlock, or vesting schedule.

## What do you need from an angel beyond capital?

> Help recruiting the first cohort of disciplined crypto traders, pressure-testing the risk and disclosure model, and connecting Gildore to product-minded Solana and perp-market operators. The right angel should improve the feedback loop, not just extend runway.
